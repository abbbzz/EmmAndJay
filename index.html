Services


1.Compliance & Basic Accounting

Regulations and financial record-keeping can feel like a constant struggle.
We relieve you of that burden.
We offer simple compliance and accounting services.
From tax filing to bookkeeping, we keep your business compliant and organized.
This lets you focus on what you do best.

2. Financial Reporting & Advisory

Managing business finances can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be.
We simplify financial reporting.
We provide clear, simple P&L statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports.
They give you a better view of your business's finances.
So, you can make decisions with confidence.
We help you manage your finances, whether you're a small Kenyan business or a global firm.

3. Budgeting & Forecasting

Every business needs a roadmap to navigate through growth, challenges, and opportunities.
Our budgeting and forecasting services help you plan.
