    Services


    1.Compliance & Basic Accounting

    Regulations and financial record-keeping can feel like a constant struggle.
    We relieve you of that burden.
    We offer simple compliance and accounting services.
    From tax filing to bookkeeping, we keep your business compliant and organized.
    This lets you focus on what you do best.

    2. Financial Reporting & Advisory

    Managing business finances can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be.
    We simplify financial reporting.
    We provide clear, simple P&L statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports.
    They give you a better view of your business's finances.
    So, you can make decisions with confidence.
    We help you manage your finances, whether you're a small Kenyan business or a global firm.
    Our insights make it easy.

    3. Budgeting & Forecasting

    Every business needs a roadmap to navigate through growth, challenges, and opportunities.
    Our budgeting and forecasting services help you plan.
    They show a snapshot of your financial future.
    We provide personalized budgets and forecasts.
    Use them to allocate resources and predict the future, local or global.

    4. Market Research

    Understanding the market is crucial for making informed decisions.
    We provide custom market research.
    It helps you understand trends, competitors, and consumers.
    We analyze relevant data to give you insights.
    They will help you stand out and succeed in your market, no matter where your business is based.

    5. Business Plan Development

    A good business plan can mean the difference between survival and success.
    We work with you to create business plans.
    They will detail your goals, strategies, and financial projections.
    We will ensure your business plan is realistic and impactful.
    Whether you want to attract investors or focus on growth.
    We have experience in many industries and geographies.
    So, we tailor every plan to your needs.
